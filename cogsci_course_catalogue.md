# Cognitive Science Honours (B.A. & Sc.) (60 credits)

**Offered by:** Science (Faculty of Science)

**Degree:** Bachelor of Arts and Science

**Program credit weight:** 60

## Program Description

The B.A. & Sc.; Honours Cognitive Science explores the multidisciplinary study of cognition in humans and machines. Topics include: the principles of intelligent behaviour, neurobiology, language, learning, and intelligent systems. The program includes an opportunity to undertake a research project under close supervision of a McGill faculty member on a topic relevant to cognitive science.

#### Degree Requirements — B.A. & Sc. students
